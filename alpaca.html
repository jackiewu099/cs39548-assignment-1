ALPACA

The alpaca is a friendly, gentle animal from the mountains of South America. With its long neck, soft fleece, and curious nature, it’s a favorite among visitors. Unlike its larger cousin, the llama, alpacas are not used for carrying loads—they’re treasured for their warm, silky wool, which people have been spinning into clothing for thousands of years.
