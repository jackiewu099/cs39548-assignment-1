HAMADRYA BABOON

The Hamadryas baboon is a large, striking primate from the rocky deserts and savannas of the Horn of Africa. Males have a silver-white mane that makes them look almost regal, while females are smaller and more subtle in color. These baboons live in complex social groups led by dominant males, with strong bonds and playful interactions.
